Sketching & Observe

Insecurities stem from a feeling of inadequacy and is something that everyone experiences. I tracked my instances of insecurity for one day, which includes what type of insecurity and if I tried to reason with myself about it.

For this data set, I tracked how my mood changes throughout one day. On this day I had some trouble working on homework. Can you tell by my frustration and sadness?

I am interested in exploring how my two cultues (American & Chinese) play a role in my life. I collected data over the course of three days of instances of when the two cultures interacted.
