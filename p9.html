Future Works

In Between is the starting point of this exploration to better understand and communicate the 1.5 generation immigrant experience. The idea of existing in between numerous cultures is a fascinating topic as it reveals nuances that make up our identity. As Iâ€™ve mainly explored the mix of Chinese and American culture, it would be interesting to include 1.5 generation immigrant perspectives from other cultures and from immigrants in other parts of the world as a part of a collective zine. Themes that emerge from this can be the point of departure to inform subsequent works to create a multimedia series around 1.5 generation immigrant experiences. Possible works include an online platform to showcase stories through videos, audio recordings, and mini games. Another possibility is to represent duality in an interactive installation. As for the current zine, I am interested in showing it to more people to see how they will react and what conversations will emerge.
